Daffodil Robotics Lab is a cutting-edge research facility located at Daffodil International University in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The lab is dedicated to advancing the field of robotics through innovative research, education, and outreach programs.

Discover more

With state-of-the-art equipment and a team of highly skilled researchers, Daffodil Robotics Lab is at the forefront of developing new technologies and applications for robotics. The lab's research focuses on various areas of robotics, including industrial automation, healthcare robotics, and educational robotics..

Discover more

In addition to research, Daffodil Robotics Lab also offers educational programs and outreach activities to engage students and the community in robotics. The lab regularly hosts workshops, seminars, and competitions to inspire young people to pursue careers in robotics and to promote awareness and understanding of the field.
